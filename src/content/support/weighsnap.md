# WeighSnap Support

Support email: [axiomplaystudios@gmail.com](mailto:axiomplaystudios@gmail.com)

WeighSnap is a local-first weight tracker for Android. If you need help, contact support at the email above and include the app version, device model, Android version, and a short description of the issue.

## Common Support Topics

### Google Drive backup and restore
- Google Drive backup is optional.
- Sign in with Google from the app's `Data & Backup` section.
- Backups are stored in your Google Drive `appDataFolder`.
- If restore fails, include the error message and whether the backup was encrypted.

### Backup encryption passphrase
- Backup encryption is optional.
- If you encrypted a backup, the same passphrase is required to restore it.
- The app does not provide passphrase recovery.
- If you forget the passphrase, the encrypted backup cannot be restored.

### Health Connect
- Health Connect sync is optional and Android-only.
- The feature requires a supported device and Health Connect installation.
- The app requests weight permissions only.
- If sync fails, include whether Health Connect is installed and whether read or write permission was granted.

### Remove Ads purchase
- `Remove Ads` is a one-time Google Play purchase.
- If your entitlement does not appear after purchase, open the app again and revisit the `Remove Ads` screen.
- Include your Play account context only if you are comfortable doing so; do not send payment card details.

### Privacy and data deletion
- Most data is stored locally on your device.
- You can delete local data in the app or by uninstalling the app.
- Google Drive backups can be deleted in the app or from your Google Drive account.
- In-app feedback records can be deleted on request by emailing support.

## Before Contacting Support

Please include:

- App version and build number
- Device manufacturer and model
- Android version
- Whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Relevant feature area, such as backup, Health Connect, reminders, billing, or app lock
